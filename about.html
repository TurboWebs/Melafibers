Why Mela Fibers

Our plant containers are made of commercial-grade fiberglass that is water, fade, and chemical-resistant. Because fiberglass planters will not rot, warp or degrade over time, they’re suitable for indoor as well as outdoor use, irrespective of weather conditions. They show virtually no signs of wear and tear even after prolonged exposure to water, UV radiation, temperature variations, salt air, and chemicals
